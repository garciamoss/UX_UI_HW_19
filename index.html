Hi, I'm Lisa.

As a freelancing Atlanta-based designer/illustrator, I have over ten years’ experience creating visually appealing illustrations, logos, printed ephemera, and websites for various small- and medium-sized businesses and nonprofits. After graduating from Georgia Tech’s UX | UI Bootcamp in June 2022, I hope to join your team as a newly minted User Experience | User Interface Designer and researcher.

UX | UI

User-centric design and experience isn’t new—Louis Sullivan’s maxim form follows function is over a century old, and applicable to disciplines from modern architecture to information architecture. My career in visual communication has led me to question the purpose of design for its intended recipients. Learning and training in UX | UI’s research and best practices have allowed me to adopt better methods to create more effective designs within a team.
